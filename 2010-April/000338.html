> How to delete a fax sent or received?

sent faxes are owned by the sender, while received faxes are owned by
the "administrative" user.

In hylafax server, you may play with a few parameters:
RecvFileMode:           0640
LogFileMode:            0640

in order to give other authorization on these files.

The key point is to create a unix group on hylafax server, and add all
users to that group.

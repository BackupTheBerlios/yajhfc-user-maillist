I am pleased to announce that version 0.3.8 of YajHFC has been released today.

Download:
http://prdownload.berlios.de/yajhfc/yajhfc-0_3_8.jar

Source code:
http://prdownload.berlios.de/yajhfc/yajhfc-0_3_8-src.zip

Windows setup.exe:
http://prdownload.berlios.de/yajhfc/yajhfc-0_3_8-setup.exe

New features/changes:
- Improved phone book window with better support for multiple phone books
- Support to save the read/unread state of faxes in a central database table
- Graphical panel to add JDBC drivers and plugins
- Russian translation of YajHFC has been added

For more information you may also take a look at the homepage at:
http://yajhfc.berlios.de/

Have fun,
Jonas

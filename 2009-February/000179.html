Am Montag, 23. Februar 2009 schrieb Vieri:
> Hi,
>
> When sending a fax with yajhfc, the "Modem" drop-down menu displays the tty
> modems and their FaxNumbers in parentheses as expected.
>
> Could it be possible to display the modem groups instead (as defined in
> ModemGroup on the Hylafax server)?

Not easily, because HylaFAX does not seem to offer a way to retrieve the 
defined ModemGroups from a client.

Thus, the only way to implement that would be to add an option to define the 
modem groups manually in YajHFC (overriding the list of modems from the 
server). 

I cannot promise that something like this will be implemented soon, however.
